F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Goffin?

Goffin is your friendly neighbour bringing you heartwarming food prepared at home. Our meals are cooked with love and packaged with care, get ready to take a trip down memory lane.

Who do we work with?

We work with our handpicked housewives who have a zeal for cooking. We help connect them to you, delivering you homecooked meals and in the process empowering them not just financially but also with another purpose and platform bringing their art of cooking to the world.

How does Goffin help you?

When you choose to order from Goffin not only are you making the right decision by investing in your health but you are also giving back to your society by supporting your local households all for meals that we provide even at less than 99 Rs.

How to be a part of our team?

Are you someone who would love to share your authentic home food and bring smiles to the faces of those away from home? If yes, then please fill out the google form given below, we are really looking forward to getting in touch with you.

Join our mission/ How you can help us?

Thank you so much for being here! Your support and kind words don’t go unnoticed. If you love what we are doing, it would mean so much to us if you could tag us on your social media platform and recommend us to a friend and anybody else who you think would be the perfect fit for our team.
