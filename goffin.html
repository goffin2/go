About Us

Home Cooks is a platform connecting home cooks with people in their community who crave home-cooked meals. Our mission is to make it easy for home cooks to sell their delicious creations, and for customers to enjoy a homemade meal without having to cook it themselves.

Our Menu

Our menu changes daily, but some of our popular dishes include:

  • Mom's Chicken Curry
  • Grandma's Lasagna
  • Dad's BBQ Ribs

We also offer a selection of desserts and appetizers. Please contact us for the daily menu.
